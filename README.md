# Shape Sorter

Pick up shapes and drop them into the matching holes on the wooden board!

https://seewhatimade.github.io/shape_sorter.github.io/

## How to Play

1. **Hover your cursor** over a shape at the bottom of the screen to pick it up (no clicking needed).
2. **Guide the shape** to the matching hole on the board above.
3. Drop it in the **correct hole** and it snaps into place with particles and a chime.
4. Drop it in the **wrong hole** and it bounces back -- try another one!
5. Each round adds more shapes to sort. See how far you can get!

## Controls

- **Mouse / Touch** -- hover over shapes to grab, move to place

## Details

- 10 shapes across rounds: circle, square, triangle, heart, star, diamond, pentagon, cross, oval, and moon
- Rounds get harder with more shapes to match
- Score and progress dots shown on screen
